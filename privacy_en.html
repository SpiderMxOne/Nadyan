Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

At Zenith AI, your privacy is our priority. This policy describes how we handle your data to provide the best wellness experience.

1. Data Collection and Use

1.1 Artificial Intelligence (Google Gemini): Zenith AI processes your text inputs to provide AI responses through Gemini 2.5 Flash. The text of your messages is sent to Google AI services to generate an empathetic response.

1.2 Health and Biometrics (Health Connect): If authorized, the app accesses heart rate data via Android Health Connect. This data is used exclusively to show your calm/stress state in real-time and suggest focus sessions. It is not stored on external servers.

1.3 Camera and Computer Vision (Breath Sync): The app requests access to the front camera exclusively for the "Breath Sync" feature. Local computer vision is used to detect your breathing rhythm. Zenith AI does not capture, record, or store images. Processing is ephemeral and 100% local.

1.4 Location: We collect your approximate location solely to obtain local weather conditions and provide context to the AI Coach.

2. Storage and Deletion

Most of your data (journals, achievements, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not require account creation, so we do not have a database with your personal identity. You can delete your data by uninstalling the app.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us through the official support of Diego Torres Barragán.
