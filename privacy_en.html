Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

At Aura Deep Work, your privacy is our priority. This policy describes how we handle your data to provide the best productivity and focus experience.

1. Data Collection and Use

1.1 Artificial Intelligence (Google Gemini): Aura Deep Work processes your text inputs to provide productivity assistance through Gemini 2.5 Flash. The text of your messages is sent to Google AI services to generate contextual responses.

1.2 Camera and Computer Vision (Flow Sync): The app requests access to the front camera exclusively for the "Flow Sync" feature. Local computer vision is used to sync visual patterns with your current rhythm. Aura Deep Work does not capture, record, or store images. Processing is ephemeral and 100% local on your device.

1.3 Location: We collect your approximate location solely to obtain local weather conditions and provide productivity context to the AI Coach.

2. Storage and Deletion

Most of your data (performance logs, achievements, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not require account creation, so we do not have a database with your personal identity. You can delete your data by uninstalling the app.

3. Medical Disclaimer

Aura Deep Work is a productivity tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide professional medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare professional for concerns related to physical or mental health.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us through the official support of Diego Torres.
