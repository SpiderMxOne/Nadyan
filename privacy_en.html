Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

At Zenith AI, your privacy is our priority. This policy describes how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

Zenith AI processes your text inputs to provide AI responses through Gemini 2.5 Flash. Additionally, the application collects approximate location data (via IP or GPS) solely to obtain local weather conditions through third-party services (such as Open-Meteo) for the Daily Wisdom feature.

2. Storage and Media

The application does not store your conversations permanently on external servers. Mood logs and achievements are stored locally on your device. During focus sessions, the application streams audio from royalty-free external sources.

3. Biometric Data

If authorized, the app accesses heart rate data via Android Health Connect. This data is processed in real-time to provide context to the AI Coach and is not shared with third parties beyond the Google AI infrastructure necessary for the service.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us through the official support of Diego Torres Barragán.
